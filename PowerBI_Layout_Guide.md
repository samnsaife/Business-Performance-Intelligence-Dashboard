# Power BI Dashboard Layout Guide (Consulting Style)

## Page 1: Executive Summary
Include KPI Cards:
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Total Orders

Add:
- Monthly Sales Trend Line Chart

---

## Page 2: Regional Performance
Charts:
- Sales by Region (Map or Bar Chart)
- Profit Leakage by Region (Negative Profit Highlight)

Key Question:
Which region needs operational optimization?

---

## Page 3: Product & Category Insights
Charts:
- Top Products by Revenue
- Category Profitability Breakdown

Key Question:
Which products drive growth and which cause loss?

---

## Page 4: Strategic Recommendations
Add a text insight panel:
- Key Findings
- Suggested Business Actions
- Growth Opportunities

Consulting Style:
Always connect data → insight → recommendation.

# Business Performance Intelligence Dashboard  
**SQL + Python ETL + Power BI + Executive KPI Analytics**

## Problem Statement
Retail organizations often struggle with profit leakage, unclear product performance,
and fragmented reporting systems.

This project delivers an executive-level Business Intelligence solution to monitor:

- Revenue growth trends  
- Profitability by region  
- Loss-making categories  
- High-value customer segments  

---

## Tech Stack
- Python (Pandas, NumPy) – ETL Automation  
- SQL – KPI & Business Performance Queries  
- Power BI – Executive Dashboard Reporting  
- Streamlit – Web-Based Recruiter Demo  
- GitHub – Portfolio Deployment  

---

## Key Business Insights
- Certain regions show consistent negative profit → operational inefficiencies  
- Category-level loss patterns suggest pricing and supply chain optimization  
- Seasonal monthly sales spikes indicate targeted promotion opportunities  
- High-value customers contribute disproportionately to revenue  

---

## Consulting Recommendations
- Optimize logistics and pricing strategy in loss-making regions  
- Prioritize top-performing product segments for growth  
- Reduce discount-driven losses in underperforming categories  
- Build retention strategies around high-value customers
